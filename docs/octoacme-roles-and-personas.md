# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers focus on user experience, ensuring features are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs. They create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications that guide development.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define design specifications and UI patterns
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature requirements
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Validate delivered features against design standards

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction scores
- Maintain consistent design language across products

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- Handoff meetings with developers
- User research findings and recommendations
- Collaboration in planning and refinement sessions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on feature requirements and user stories; validate designs meet business objectives
- **Developers**: Provide design specifications and assets; review implementation for design fidelity
- **Customer Support Liaison**: Gather feedback on user pain points; incorporate support insights into design improvements
- **Project Managers**: Participate in planning; communicate design timeline and dependencies

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate and orchestrate the release process, ensuring smooth deployments and minimizing risk. They maintain release schedules, coordinate across teams, and ensure all release requirements are met.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and coordinate release schedules and deployment windows
- Manage release branches and version control strategy
- Ensure all pre-release requirements are met (testing, documentation, approvals)
- Coordinate deployment activities and monitor release health
- Maintain release notes and changelog
- Facilitate go/no-go decisions for releases

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, on-schedule releases with minimal incidents
- Reduce deployment risk through proper planning and coordination
- Maintain clear communication about release status and changes

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings and go/no-go reviews
- Deployment notifications and status updates
- Release notes and stakeholder communications
- Post-release reports and metrics

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Coordinate code freeze and release branch management; ensure CI/CD pipeline health
- **Product Managers**: Align release timing with feature priorities and market requirements
- **Project Managers**: Coordinate release schedules with project timelines; report on release status
- **Customer Support Liaison**: Communicate release changes and prepare support team for new features
- **UX Designer**: Ensure design assets and documentation are ready for release

---

## Customer Support Liaison

### Role Summary
Customer Support Liaisons bridge the gap between users and the development team. They gather customer feedback, escalate issues, and ensure the team understands user needs and pain points.

### Responsibilities
- Collect and prioritize customer feedback and feature requests
- Escalate critical issues and bugs to development team
- Provide context on customer impact for prioritization decisions
- Create and maintain support documentation and FAQs
- Communicate product updates to support team
- Validate that fixes resolve customer-reported issues

### Goals
- Ensure customer voice influences product decisions
- Reduce time-to-resolution for customer issues
- Improve customer satisfaction and reduce support burden

### Typical Communication
- Weekly feedback summaries and trend reports
- Bug reports and escalation notices
- Support documentation and knowledge base updates
- Release readiness briefings for support team

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Share customer insights and feature requests; help prioritize based on customer impact
- **Developers**: Report bugs with customer context; validate fixes in staging environments
- **UX Designer**: Provide user feedback and pain points to inform design decisions
- **Release Manager**: Coordinate on release communications and support readiness
- **Project Managers**: Report on customer-impacting issues; participate in retrospectives with customer perspective
